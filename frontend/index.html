NinebyNine Sudoku AI

Foundations of AI - Sudoku as a CSP

This AI Sudoku game is an interactive, educational tool designed to help you understand how common AI solving techniques work. Instead of instantly filling in the board, it shows how algorithms like backtracking, MRV (Minimum Remaining Values), forward checking, and even local search approaches operate step by step. You can watch how each method makes decisions, handles constraints, and solves the puzzle in real time. The goal is to give you a clear, visual understanding of how AI solvers actually think while working through a problem like Sudoku.

Click a difficulty level to start playing. When you get stuck, you can use the AI solver to watch how it solves the puzzle.
